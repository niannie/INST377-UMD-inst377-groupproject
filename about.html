SnackTrack will help users monitor their fitness journeys by providing an accessible platform to log their food, exercise, and overall health metrics, allowing them to achieve their fitness goals. Our proposed system will allow users to input personal details such as their daily caloric intake and exercise routines, and track them over time. The app will provide an easy-to-navigate interface where users can log their meals and workouts, receive feedback, and view progress through attractive visualizations. We will use the Sport and Fitness API to access reliable nutrition and fitness data for food items and exercises. This will simplify the process for users by providing a comprehensive database for tracking and storing their data.
