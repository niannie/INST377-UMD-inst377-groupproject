Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use SnackTrack.

Information We Collect

  • Personal details like your name, email address, and account information.
  • Data related to your health and activity logs, including age, height, and weight. This information is used to calculate metrics such as Body Mass Index (BMI) and Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR), which help us provide you with personalized fitness and nutrition recommendations.
  • Technical information such as your IP address, browser type, and cookies.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our services, including personalized health and fitness recommendations.
  • To calculate and track health metrics like BMI and BMR to help you reach your fitness goals.
  • To communicate updates, promotions, and other information related to your account.
  • To ensure the security of your account and prevent fraudulent activity.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your data. We may share your information with trusted third parties only to provide the services you use or as required by law. Your information is never shared or sold to external parties for marketing purposes.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights or if you have any concerns, please contact our support team at support@snacktrack.com.
